MSM Concussion Assessment

Orientation Maddock's

  • Where are we?
  • What quarter (half/period) is it right now?
  • Who scored last in practice/game?
  • Who did we play in the last game?
  • Did we win the last game?

Word Recall SAC

Read list of words one second at a time and ask to repeat list in any order. Repeat same list with same instructions for trial 2 and 3. Do not advise there will be a delayed recall.

Word List:
  • Bacon
  • Hunter
  • Paper
  • Silver
  • Rabbit

Delayed Work Recall

Perform at end of all sideline testing, at least >5 minutes

Concentration SAC

Read string of numbers, ask athlete to repeat backwards. If correct, go to next string length; if incorrect, go to next string, same length.

Sequence
  • 3-1-6
  • 4-8-3-7
  • 5-1-6-9-2
  • 8-4-2-6-5-8

12 Months-in-reverse Order

Recite the 12 months of the year backwards in <30 seconds

Modified Bess

Three 20-second tests, eyes closed, hands on waist

Tests:
  • Double leg stance (even momentary)
  • Single leg stance (non-dominant foot)
  • Tandem stance (non-dominant foot at back)

Coordination

Tests:
  • Upper extremity (right or left)
  • Finger-to-nose, eyes open
  • Five repetitions in < 4 seconds = 1 point, failure = 0
