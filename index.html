Modified Bess

Three 20-second tests, eyes closed, hands on waist

Types of Errors:
  • Opening eyes
  • Step, stumble or fall
  • Hands lifted off waist
  • Lifting forefoot or heel
  • Remaining out of test position >5 secs
  • Moving hip into >30 degrees abduction
Tests:
  • Double leg stance (even momentary)
  • Single leg stance (non-dominant foot)
  • Tandem stance (non-dominant foot at back)

Coordination

Five repetitions in <4 seconds = 1 point, failure - 0. Must touch nose, arm must exten each time.

Tests:
  • Upper extremity (right or left)
  • Finger-to-nose, eyes open
